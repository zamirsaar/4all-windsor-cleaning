Flexible Rates & Special Deals

At 4All Windsor Cleaning Services, we keep pricing transparent and fair. Our base rate is $40/hour per cleaner. If two cleaners come, they'll finish in half the time, but total cost typically remains the same (2 x $40/hr).

Use our quick calculator for a rough estimate, or explore our Promotions on the right for extra savings!

Approx. Hour Guidelines

Single-person hours give you a reference. A 3-bed home might be 4-5 hours for one cleaner (or 2-2.5 hours if two come).

Home Size Single-Person Hours
1-2 Beds (under 1,000 sq ft) 2-3 hours
3 Beds (1,000-1,500 sq ft) 3-5 hours
4 Beds (1,500-2,000 sq ft) 5-7 hours
5+ Beds (2,000+ sq ft) 7+ hours
*Deep cleans, move-out, or post-construction may add extra hours.

What Affects the Price?

  • Square Footage & Rooms: Bigger space, more hours.
  • Type of Cleaning: Deep, Move-In/Out, Post-Construction need added time.
  • Pets & Clutter: Pet hair or heavy tidying adds effort.
  • Add-Ons: Ovens, fridges, interior windows, etc.
  • Frequency: Routine clients see fewer hours & costs after the first visit.

More Services & Discounts

  • Recurring Plans: Weekly/biweekly cleaning can unlock extra loyalty discounts.
  • Move-In/Out Cleans: Great for leaving a place spotless or settling in fresh.
  • Post-Renovation: We handle leftover dust, debris, and paint splatter.
  • Custom Schedules: We tailor to your needs—just ask!
