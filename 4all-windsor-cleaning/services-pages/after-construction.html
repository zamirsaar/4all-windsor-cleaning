A Fresh Start

Post-construction or renovation dust and debris can be overwhelming. Our After Construction Cleaning focuses on removing that fine layer of dust, leftover materials, and general buildup so your newly remodeled or built space is truly move-in ready.

Why Choose Our Post-Construction Service?

  • Safe & Dust-Free: We clear the microscopic dust that can linger in vents, corners, and surfaces.
  • Protect New Surfaces: Proper cleaning prevents scratches or damage to freshly installed floors, tiles, or fixtures.
  • Saves You Time: Avoid the headache of sweeping endless dust; our team handles everything swiftly.

How We Do It

  1. Site Inspection: We identify major debris, dust hotspots, and newly installed surfaces requiring gentle care.
  2. Dust & Debris Removal: Using professional vacuums and damp-wiping methods, we capture fine particles to prevent them from resettling.
  3. Detailed Surface Clean: All countertops, fixtures, baseboards, and floors are carefully sanitized and polished.
  4. Finishing Touches: We check windowsills, light fixtures, and any leftover smudges or adhesives on surfaces.

Typical Checklist

  • Remove and discard leftover materials, small debris, or packaging
  • Vacuum and wipe down walls, ceilings, and trim to remove fine dust
  • Clean windows, frames, and any glass doors
  • Sanitize bathrooms (toilet, tub, sink, tiles)
  • Wipe and sanitize kitchen surfaces, cabinets, appliance exteriors
  • Mop or steam-clean floors to remove hidden grime
