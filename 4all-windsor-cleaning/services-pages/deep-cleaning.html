Deep Cleaning

Give Your Home the Ultimate Refresh, Top to Bottom

Book Now

Transform Your Space

Our Deep Cleaning service is a comprehensive, top-to-bottom approach that leaves every inch of your home sparkling. Ideal for seasonal refreshes, special occasions, or whenever your space needs extra care. We’ll tackle hidden dirt, buildup, and hard-to-reach corners so you can enjoy a truly rejuvenated home.

What Is Deep Cleaning?

  • Intensive Surface Cleaning: We focus on neglected areas like baseboards, window tracks, under furniture, and more.
  • Detail-Oriented: Scrub grout, remove cobwebs, and polish fixtures for that brand-new feeling.
  • Appliance Interiors: Optional fridge, oven, or cabinet interior cleaning to eliminate hidden germs or residue.

Why Deep Cleaning Matters

  • Health & Hygiene: Eradicate bacteria, allergens, and mold lurking behind or beneath surfaces.
  • Prolong Your Interiors: Regular deep cleans extend the life of your floors, carpets, and furnishings.
  • Peace of Mind: Enjoy total freshness, knowing every corner has been meticulously sanitized.

Our Deep Cleaning Process

  1. Consultation: We assess your home’s needs, identifying problem areas that need extra attention.
  2. Customized Plan: We tailor our checklist to address high-traffic zones, hidden buildup, and any special requests (like oven or fridge interiors).
  3. In-Depth Clean: Our team uses professional-grade products and tools to lift deep-seated dirt, sanitize surfaces, and polish fixtures.
  4. Final Inspection: We walk through each room, ensuring we meet (or exceed) your expectations.

Deep Cleaning Highlights

Detailed Dusting

Wipe down vents, ceiling fans, light fixtures, and cabinet tops.

Kitchen & Appliances

Thoroughly sanitize sinks, countertops, and backsplash. Optional interior cleaning of oven and fridge.

Bathroom Overhaul

Scrub grout, disinfect toilets, tubs, and showers, plus polish mirrors and fixtures.

Floor Care

Vacuum edges, steam-mop tiles (optional), and spot-clean carpets.

Door & Trim

Sanitize door handles, light switches, and thoroughly wipe door frames and baseboards.
